About Aurangabad

Aurangabad, officially known as Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, is one of Maharashtra's most famous tourist destinations. The city is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, ancient caves, forts, and beautiful monuments.

It is home to the world-famous Ajanta and Ellora Caves, both recognized as UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Visitors from around the world come to experience its architecture, history, and traditions.

Apart from historical attractions, Aurangabad is also known for local handicrafts, delicious cuisine, and vibrant culture, making it a perfect destination for travelers.
